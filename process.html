How We Work With You

Five steps. A written plan. And an ongoing relationship that evolves as your life does.

Step 1: Understanding Your Life & Goals

~45 minutes  ·  Video or in-person

Before we talk about any investment, we listen. We want to understand your complete financial picture — your income, your family, your obligations, and the future you are working towards:

  • Complete financial life assessment — income, expenses, existing assets
  • Short, medium, and long-term goal mapping
  • Honest risk tolerance evaluation
  • Identification of insurance gaps and liquidity needs
A product chosen without understanding your life is just a guess. We refuse to guess with your future.

Step 2: Building Your Financial Plan

Delivered within 5 business days  ·  Written plan document

Every client receives a personalised written financial plan before a single rupee is invested. This is not a fund shortlist — it is a comprehensive strategy document that covers:

  • Goal-wise corpus targets and timelines
  • Recommended asset allocation with clear rationale
  • Tax optimisation strategy — ELSS, capital gains, regime selection
  • Insurance coverage assessment and gaps
Most advisors skip straight to product recommendations. We think that is backwards. The plan comes first — always.

Step 3: Portfolio Construction & Fund Selection

Presented for your review before anything is actioned

With the financial plan as our foundation, we select the specific instruments to bring it to life. Our research goes well beyond star ratings:

  • 7-year rolling returns and full market cycle performance
  • Fund manager tenure, philosophy, and consistency
  • Downside capture and behaviour during 2008, 2013, 2020 crashes
  • Expense ratio, portfolio overlap, and tax efficiency
You will never receive a recommendation you do not understand. We explain every choice in plain language — what it is, why it fits your plan, and what to realistically expect from it.

Step 4: Implementation

Up and running within a few days  ·  We handle the paperwork

Once you are comfortable with the plan, we take care of every detail to get things moving:

  • KYC and account opening assistance
  • SIP and lump sum investment setup
  • Nomination, beneficiary, and succession documentation
  • Coordination with your CA or tax advisor where needed
For many clients, this is their first time investing formally. We make sure the experience feels guided and simple — not like navigating a government office alone.

Step 5: Ongoing Monitoring & Review

Quarterly reviews  ·  Proactive guidance at every life stage

A financial plan is not a document you file away. Life changes — and your strategy must change with it. We stay actively involved through every milestone:

  • Quarterly portfolio reviews with plain-language summaries
  • Rebalancing when markets shift your allocation off-target
  • Annual financial plan review — goals, insurance, tax strategy
  • Proactive outreach at key life events — not just at review time
When the Sensex drops 1,500 points and your phone is full of WhatsApp forwards, you will hear from us first — with context, calm, and a clear recommendation. That is what a wealth manager actually does.
