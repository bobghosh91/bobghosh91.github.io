About Earthen Capital

Helping investors achieve their financial goals through expert investment solutions since 2025.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Earthen began with a simple mission: to help individuals and families build wealth through smart, disciplined mutual fund investing.

As a newly established firm led by young professionals, our mission is simple: to provide clear, honest, and effective investment guidance that helps clients move closer to their financial goals.

Our Mission

To provide unbiased, research-driven mutual fund advice that helps clients achieve their financial goals with confidence.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted mutual fund distribution partner for investors seeking long-term wealth creation.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make

Client First

We always put our clients' interests first, providing recommendations based on what's best for their financial situation, not our bottom line.

Research Driven

Our investment recommendations are backed by rigorous research and analysis of fund performance, management, and strategy.

Transparency

We believe in full disclosure of fees, potential conflicts, and the reasoning behind our investment recommendations.

Education

We empower our clients with knowledge, helping them understand their investments and make informed decisions.

Teamwork

We collaborate across disciplines to provide comprehensive financial solutions tailored to each client's unique needs.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from client service to investment research and portfolio construction.
